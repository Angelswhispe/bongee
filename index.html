roadmap

Project Launch

February 2023

The art component of the project has been developed and finalized, and the initial stages of NFT token creation have begun.

Development of our unique NFT collection

March 2023

Create the artistic foundation of the project, develop the design of the NFT collection.

Website and branding development

April 2023

Website development and coordinated branding strategy has begun, online presence and community building around the project has increased.

Website and Twitter account launch

May 2023

Official website launch in conjunction with the creation of a Twitter account, creating new channels for community interaction and updates.

Game development begins

July 2023

Initiation of the game development process, meticulous planning and execution of the intended concept, the project enters the active production phase.

Creation of the white paper, launch of Discord, Telegram, Instagram and Zealy

September 2023

Preparation of the company's White Paper, which clearly outlines the project's goals, plans, and technical aspects in front of an audience, as well as the launch of various social platforms and integration with Zealy to increase reach and community engagement.

Launch of an active advertising campaign.

October 2023

An extensive publicity campaign is launched to attract an audience. Various platforms are used to interact with potential project participants.

Alpha collection development

November 2023

The project enters the active development phase of the Alpha Collection, preparing the public for its minting.

Minting the Alpha Collection, supporting an animal shelter

March 2024

Minting of the Alpha Collection, conducting sales tests and gathering active feedback.

Opening of chats for alpha collection owners

March 2024

Opening of exclusive chats for Alpha Collection owners, creating a space for discussion and networking among early supporters of the project.

New Advertising Campaign and Preparation for Main Collection Release

April 2024

Creation of a new advertising campaign to inform the public about the date of the public minting of the main collection and to share information about unique offers.

Active creation of the main collection whitelist

May 2024

The most active community members will have the first access to the exclusive collection before the official start of the public minting of the NFT collection.

Minting of the main collection

June 2024

Minting of the 3D NFT collection "Base Chocs". Main NFT Collection holders will receive exclusive rewards and bonuses in the project, and the owners of both alpha and main collection

Launch of new chats for collection owners, start of active game development phase

July 2024

Further development of effective communication with interested project participants, release of exclusive materials for the most active community members. Main NFT Collection holders will get early access to the game

We launch an ambassador program for cooperation with us.

August 2024

As part of this program, projects will be able to submit applications to join our program and actively work with our project.

Release of the proprietary web3 Play2Earn game "Base Chocs"

December 2024

The long-awaited release of the web3 proprietary game "Base Chocs", an active advertising campaign and expansion of the "Base Chocs" ecosystem.

Non-stop development!
